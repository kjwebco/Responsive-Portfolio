About Me

I love learning and want to always be teachable. I can build stand alone micro-sites or collaborate with others on large projects. I seek out opportunities continuing to take big ideas and turn them into reality with dedicated, passionate, and playful teams. I want to be a design leader taking on challenging projects with elegant solutions. My colleagues describe me as energetic and conscientious. I am a highly self-motivated person who constantly strives to be the best at what I am doing and will endlessly raise the bar for any challenge put in front of me. I find that solving new programming challenges or learning new languages and frameworks gives me a spark of excitement that naturally motivates me to keep pushing myself further.
